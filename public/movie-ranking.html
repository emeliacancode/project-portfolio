Top Rated Movies According to Emelia

My Top Favorite Movies of All Time!

The Notebook

Great romance about how love can stand the tests of times.

Titanic

Finding love unexpectedly in random places despite status or class.

The Curious Case of Benjamin Button

About a man who ages backwards.... essentially born old and wrinkled.

The Age of Adaline

About a women who never ages! Can you imagine never aging?
